KeenKeeper helps you keep track of your friendships and social connections. It's designed to remind you when you last spoke to someone and helps you maintain meaningful relationships without losing touch.

* Features:-
Friend Dashboard: View all your friends in a clean grid layout with status indicators (Overdue, Almost Due, On-track).
Detailed Profiles: See specific information about each friend, including their bio, tags, and contact goals.
Interaction Logging: Log calls, texts, or video chats with a single click.
Timeline: A complete history of all your logged interactions with filtering options.
Analytics: Visual breakdown of your communication habits (Call vs Text vs Video).
Responsive Design: Works perfectly on mobile, tablet, and desktop screens.

* Tech Stack:-
Frontend: React.js, Tailwind CSS
Routing: React Router DOM
Icons & Assets: React Icons, Custom PNG icons
Toast: React Hot Toast
Data Handling: Local Storage
